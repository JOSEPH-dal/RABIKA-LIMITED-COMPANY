About Rabika Ltd

Rabika Limited Company is a boutique business located in Musanze – Cyuve, Rwanda, providing essential food and household products in bulk. Our key goal is to serve the local market with reliable supply, quality products, and honest service.

Vision

To be a trusted supplier of food and basic needs in Northern Rwanda and beyond.

Mission

Delivering reliable and affordable wholesale services for schools, homes, and retail shops with integrity and speed.

Leadership

Rabika Ltd is managed by Mr. Joseph Irakoze, a committed and visionary entrepreneur serving as Managing Director.
